Simulation Setup

Explore an infinite grid and watch Langton's Ant emerge from simple rules.

How It Works:

  • Click Start Infinite Grid to begin at the origin.
  • Drag the grid to scroll around the infinite plane.
  • Use the mouse wheel or trackpad to zoom in and out.
  • Adjust the tickspeed slider to control simulation speed.
