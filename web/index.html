Simulation Setup

Configure your grid and watch Langton's Ant emerge from simple rules.

How It Works

  • 1 Enter your desired grid width and height.
  • 2 Choose whether the edges wrap around or act as hard walls.
  • 3 Click Submit to start the simulation.
  • 4 Adjust the tickspeed slider to control simulation speed.
