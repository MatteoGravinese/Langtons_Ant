Langton's Ant Simulator

by Matteo Gravinese

Simulation Setup

Configure the ant's colour rules, then run the simulation and watch complex behaviour emerge from simple logic.

How to Use:

  • Set your colour rules below, then click Start Simulation.
  • Click and drag to pan around the infinite grid.
  • Scroll or pinch to zoom in and out.
  • Use Step Delay to control how fast the ant moves.
